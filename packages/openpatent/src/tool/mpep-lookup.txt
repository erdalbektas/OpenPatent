Look up Manual of Patent Examining Procedure (MPEP) sections.

Search the MPEP by section number or keyword to find relevant patent examination guidance.

Parameters:
- section: MPEP section number (e.g., "2143", "706.07(a)")
- keyword: Search keyword or phrase (e.g., "obviousness", "written description")
- context: Brief description of why you need this lookup (helps refine results)

Returns the relevant MPEP text, key holdings, and related sections.
